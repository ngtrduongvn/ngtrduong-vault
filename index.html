Welcome to NTD Vault, a place dedicated to preserving and exploring the entire Minecraft journey of Nguyen Trung Duong. This website was created as a personal archive, where I record and share everything connected to Minecraft - from mods I've developed, to creative builds of all scales, and the memorable adventures I've had in survival worlds.

Over time, NTD Vault has grown into a digital legacy, not just a collection of projects, but a reflection of my growth and my enduring passion for Minecraft. Every milestone, every idea, and every experiment is kept here as part of the journey.

If you are new here, a great place to start is the Archive page, where you can revisit the projects, moments, and creations that have shaped my Minecraft path.
